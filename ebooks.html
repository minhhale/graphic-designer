Digital Books & Storytelling

I design and produce Radio Free Asia’s eBooks, turning investigative reporting and cultural stories into engaging, easy-to-read digital publications. I love shaping clean typography, custom graphics, and responsive layouts that bring important stories to life and make them easy for anyone to access. My projects include Drawing Fire, an SDX Award winner, available as PDF, ePub, and interactive downloads on iTunes and Google Play.

Drawing Fire: The Political Cartoons of Rebel Pepper eBook

Project Overview

This award-winning eBook showcases the daring political cartoons of Rebel Pepper, a Chinese dissident artist known for sharp commentary on censorship, authoritarianism, and global politics. Published by Radio Free Asia, the project aimed to preserve and share Rebel Pepper’s body of work in a format that is both visually striking and easy to navigate. The eBook went on to win an SDX Award for Excellence in Journalism, recognizing its creative storytelling and impact.

Design Goals
  • • Visual Storytelling: Present hundreds of cartoons in a way that honors their artistry and political weight.
  • • User-Friendly Reading: Deliver a seamless digital reading experience across desktop and mobile.
  • • Brand Alignment: Reflect RFA’s commitment to press freedom and creative expression.
Process & Tools

I designed the eBook layout using InDesign and coordinated with editors and Rebel Pepper to organize the cartoons by theme and chronology. I applied a restrained color palette—anchored in Rebel Pepper’s signature blues—to create a gallery-like feel, while using bold typography and high-resolution images to preserve each cartoon’s detail. Interactive PDF features and optimized file sizes ensured smooth downloads and sharing across global audiences.

Outcome

The final eBook not only preserved Rebel Pepper’s work but also elevated it, earning the SDX Award for its innovative fusion of art and journalism. Readers can explore the cartoons as both political critique and cultural record, amplifying awareness of press freedom and resistance to censorship.
